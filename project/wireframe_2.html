Things to Do

Nantucket is more than just an Island - here are the top three things to do when you are here!

Surfside Beach

Beach

Only being a 10 minute drive from town, Surfside Beach is one Nantucket's finest. Waves reach up to 10 feet which make it perfect for surfer lovers; hence the name. There is also plenty of parking as well as public bathrooms and a snack bar. 

Deep Sea Fishing

activity

Deep Sea Fishing is a great family-friendly activity. There are multiple companies that offer their services right in town - just pick one and hope for a catch!

Cisco Brewery

entertainment

Cisco Brewery is Nantucket's first and only craft brewery, about 5 minutes from town. They offer their own homemade drinks accompanied with live music and food trucks. 21+

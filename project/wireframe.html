Welcome!

This website was created to go you insight into your trip before you even get there - specifically activities and dining options.

Nantucket Island is located 30 miles off the coast of Cape Cod. The Island itself is known for or whaling, Nantucket bay scallops, foggy weather, lighthouses (three of them!), Main Street, Cisco Brewers, fantastic fishing, iconic holidays like Nantucket Stroll and the Daffodil Festival and the Rainbow Fleet. It has become an increasingly popular summer destination for people all over the country the past few years. Just an hour ferry ride away, Nantucket has the perfectly windswept beach with lighthouses that look like they come straight from a postcard.
